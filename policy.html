Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Uma Laserworks ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use or make a purchase from www.umalaserworks.in (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, make a purchase, or contact us. This may include your name, email, address, phone number, payment information, and other related data.

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our Site, including your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing activity through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your transactions and send confirmations
  • Communicate with you, including responding to inquiries and sending updates
  • Personalize your experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or other illegal activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with service providers that assist with payment processing, shipping, analytics, customer service, and marketing. We may also share information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our Site. Cookies help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our Site, and tailor advertisements. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. Children

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@umalaserworks.in.
